<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| persisted proposal facts -> pending ticket | Only a durable candidate plus complete fresh evidence and accepted risk assessment may issue a pending ticket. |
| pending ticket -> outbound boundary | Ticket issue and lifecycle are not authority to admit a command or submit to a gateway. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-02-10 | Tampering | `ApprovalTicket` binding and freshness | high | mitigate | Fixed TTL, immutable digest bindings, fresh precheck, and D-10 invalidation tests prevent replayed or changed facts from remaining current. |
| T-02-11 | Repudiation | ticket terminal lifecycle | medium | mitigate | Append-only reject, expire, and invalidation facts retain reason, timestamp, actor label, and binding snapshot under D-12. |
| T-02-13 | Tampering | automatic ticket issue and fee review | high | mitigate | Candidate/assessment uniqueness plus target/product/symbol/quote/rate-version-bound Decimal fee facts make D-04 issuance idempotent and reject missing or mismatched evidence. |
| T-02-15 | Elevation | proposal-to-ticket wiring | high | mitigate | Integration tests prove automatic issuance creates no admission, `OutboundSubmission`, submission-coordinator call, or gateway call; 02-07 is the only authorization owner. |
| T-02-SC | Tampering | package installs | low | accept | No package-manager installation is planned; existing Python, SQLite, Pytest, and Hypothesis dependencies are used. |
</threat_model>
